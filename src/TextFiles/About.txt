 Welcome to HuntGame!
 HuntGame is a captivating Java-based strategy game where you,
 as the hunter, aim to catch a dynamic target on the game board.

 About the Development:
 Crafted with Java and Swing,
 HuntGame offers a seamless and visually engaging user interface.
